## Project Overview
This project focuses on building a **Digital Literacy Portfolio** to develop essential skills needed in today's digital world. It includes creating a professional online presence, understanding digital tools, communicating properly, and ensuring online safety.

The project is split into five modules (tasks), each addressing an important area of digital literacy such as awareness, portfolio building, collaboration tools, communication etiquette, and preventing cybercrime.

## Introduction
As a Student Digital Ambassador, I am working on this project to promote digital literacy and responsible online behavior among my peers. In today's technology-driven world, people often interact with various digital platforms. However, many lack a solid understanding of how to use them effectively, ethically, and securely.

This project focuses on developing a Digital Literacy Portfolio that showcases key skills like building a professional online presence, using digital tools effectively, and maintaining proper online communication.

The project has five specific tasks, each covering a crucial aspect of digital literacy. These include creating awareness, developing a portfolio, exploring platforms, email etiquette, and preventing cybercrime. Through this initiative, I aim to raise digital awareness and prepare students for success in their academic and professional lives.
